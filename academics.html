Academics

Kings College Budo is committed to academic excellence through rigorous teaching, strong values, and a balanced curriculum that prepares learners for lifelong success.

Our Programs

Explore the two main academic pathways offered at the school.

O-Level (Lower Secondary)

A strong lower secondary foundation focused on core competencies, character development, and preparation for national examinations.

Duration: 4 years (Senior 1 - Senior 4)

Sample Subjects: Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, ICT

A-Level (Upper Secondary)

A specialized upper secondary curriculum that deepens subject mastery, critical thinking, and university/career readiness.

Duration: 2 years (Senior 5 - Senior 6)

Sample Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, ICT, Economics
